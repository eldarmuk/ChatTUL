Meet the student clubs

Iron Warriors team


Their famous car drove 837 kilometers on one liter of fuel and set a Polish record. The team's current third vehicle made its debut with a victory at the Shell Eco-marathon France 2018 in Paris in the Prototype Gasoline category. The team took first place at the Educ Eco competition in France and became the fastest growing Polish team competing in Eco-marathons.

Image
More

Lodz Solar Team

The Lodz Solar Team is a team of TUL students, but above all enthusiasts and builders of solar-powered electric cars. The Eagle One and Eagle Two city cars built by the team make practical use of solar energy and take part in competitions around the world - and win! And, just as importantly, they support urban energy.

Image
More

ESN-EYE

ESN-EYE is a self-governing organization that has been operating at Lodz University of Technology for more than 20 years - defending its title as the best section in the country for the past two years. It focuses on international activities, mainly working with students coming to Poland from the Erasmus+ program.
